## [2356.每位教师所教授的科目种类的数量 中文热门题解1](https://leetcode.cn/problems/number-of-unique-subjects-taught-by-each-teacher/solutions/100000/di-yi-ge-ti-jie-taigaox-by-jerryluan-7tea)
